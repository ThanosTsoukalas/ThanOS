ThanOS saves all your files, documents and preferences on local folders, and thus does not collect them nor share them with any Third Party. Telemetry data are also not collected. ThanOS also does not collect any data about purchases or transactions within its Platform and does not share information with advertisers or trackers.
With Quick Setup, you can import Configuration Files to Set Up ThanOS however you like in a couple of seconds. If you don't have any Configuration Files, click 'No Thanks'.
